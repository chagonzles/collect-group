About us

We believe that individuals and small business should have the same services and opportunities as large companies. The Collect Group supports the home senders and heroes on the high street, by allowing them to process shipments cost effectively whilst using innovative IT solutions. This not only saves money, but also frees up valuable time so they can focus on the more important things in their lives.

Our approach is fun and honest, with “LEFT FIELD” thinking, whilst avoiding the corporate stereotypes of the courier world.
